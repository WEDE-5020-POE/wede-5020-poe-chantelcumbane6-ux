## Project Overview

Chanty Cakes, founded in 2020 by Chantel Cumbane during the COVID-19 pandemic, began as a home-based baking hobby and has grown into a recognized local business known for its creative designs, quality ingredients, and personalized service. Specializing in custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, and special occasions, the business aims to provide affordable, freshly baked products that bring people together while using locally sourced ingredients. Its vision is to become a leading bakery brand within the community by consistently delivering beautiful and delicious creations that exceed customer expectations. The primary target market includes parents of young children, young adults seeking trendy cakes, and couples planning intimate weddings. This project focuses on developing a website to enhance online visibility, showcase products, facilitate customer orders, and strengthen brand awareness, with success measured through website orders, customer enquiries, and growth in social media engagement.
## Website Goals and Objectives
 To create online visibility of the business, showcasing products and allowing customers to place orders anytime and building brand awareness also for announcing promotions online.
