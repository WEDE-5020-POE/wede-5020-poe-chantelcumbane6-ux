Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I place an order for a custom cake?

A: You can place an order for a custom cake by filling out our online enquiry form on the Enquiry page or by contacting us directly through phone or email. Our team will be happy to assist you with your order and answer any questions you may have.

Q: What is the lead time for custom cake orders?

A: We recommend placing your custom cake order at least 7 days in advance to ensure we have enough time to create your perfect cake. However, we will do our best to accommodate last-minute orders whenever possible.

Q: Do you offer delivery services?

A: Yes, we offer delivery services for our cakes. Delivery fees may apply based on the location and size of the cake. Please contact us for more information about our delivery options.

Q: Can I request a specific flavor or design for my cake?

A: Absolutely! We offer a wide range of flavors and designs to choose from, and we can also create custom cakes tailored to your specific preferences. Just let us know your ideas, and our team will work with you to bring your vision to life.

Q: What are your payment options?

A: We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments. Payment details will be provided when you place your order.

Q: Do you accommodate dietary restrictions or allergies?

A: Yes, we can accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies. Please let us know about any specific requirements when placing your order, and we will do our best to create a cake that meets your needs.
