Our History

Chanty cakes was established in 2020 by Chantel Cumbane,a dedicated home baker who discovered her passion during the COVID-19 pandemic.What began as a hobby baking for family soon evolved into a business, supported by encouragement from friends and the local community. Initially focusing on birthday cakes.Chanty Cakes gained recognition through social media and word of mouth for distinctive designs, quality ingredients and personalized services. Today, the business specializes in custom-made cakes for birthdays, weddings and special occasions.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide our community with affordable, freshly baked cakes that provide flavor ,quality and hospitality.And to bring people together with our crafted fresh cakes using locally sourced ingredients.

Our vision is to be recognized as a leading bakery brand in our community, by delivering beautifully delicious baked creations that exceed expectations that our customers can always count on.
